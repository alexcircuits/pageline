Features

Everything a reader needs.

From a private library to a daily streak tracker — built for people who actually finish books.

Live Discord Rich Presence

Your friends see what book you're reading — title, author, current page, and how long you've been at it.

Google Books search

Search millions of titles. Add covers and metadata in one click — no manual entry, no fluff.

Reading stats & streaks

Daily, weekly, and monthly time. Pages read, books started, and a streak counter to keep you honest.

Apple Books detection

On macOS, Pageline can read your active title straight from the Books app — zero typing.

Daily reading goals

Set a target in minutes. Watch your progress fill up. Build the habit you've been meaning to.

Personal library

Save books you're reading, want to read, or have finished. Add covers from disk or paste a URL.

Cross-platform & private

Mac, Windows, and Linux. All data lives on your machine — no sign-up, no telemetry, ever.

CSV export

Export every reading session to CSV and bring your data anywhere. It's yours.

Setup in 2 minutes

How it works

Connect once. Pick a book. Start reading. That's it.

Install & open

Download Pageline for your OS and launch the app.

Connect Discord

Paste a free Discord Application ID into Settings and click Connect.

Pick a book

Search Google Books, import from Apple Books, or add your own.

Hit Start Reading

Your status goes live. Pages and timer update automatically.
